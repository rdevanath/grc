# Generally Accepted Privacy Principles (GAPP)

Generally Accepted Privacy Principles (GAPP) is a comprehensive privacy framework _developed jointly_ by the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants (AICPA) and the Canadian Institute of Chartered Accountants (CICA).

It’s **10 principles** guides organizations—especially accounting firms and service providers—in designing, implementing, and maintaining effective privacy programs that manage personal information ethically and in line with legal requirements. **Mandatory for publicly traded U.S. companies.**

Framework Structure - GAPP is built on a single overarching privacy objective. Personal information must be collected, used, retained, and disclosed in accordance with the commitments in the organization’s privacy notice and with criteria defined in the GAPP framework.

This objective is supported by 10 Privacy Principles:

1. **Management:** Defining and assigning accountability for privacy policies.

2. **Notice & Communication:** Informing individuals about privacy policies and the purposes of data collection.

3. **Choice and consent:** Providing individuals with options regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of their data.

4. **Collection:** Limiting data collection to only what is necessary.

5. **Use, retention and disposal:** Limiting the use of data to the stated purposes, retaining it only as long as necessary, and then disposing of it securely.

6. **Access:** Giving individuals access to their personal information for review and updates.

7. **Disclosure to third parties:** Limiting disclosure to third parties and requiring individual consent.

8. **Security for privacy:** Protecting personal information with appropriate security measures.

9. **Quality:** Maintaining accurate, complete, and relevant personal information.

10. **Monitoring and enforcement:** Monitoring compliance and having procedures to address complaints.

**Implementation and Benefits**

* **Privacy Program Design:** Use GAPP’s objectives and criteria to build policies, procedures, and controls.

* **SOC 2 Integration:** GAPP aligns directly with SOC 2’s Privacy Trust Services Criteria, enabling streamlined audits.

* **Global Compliance:** Provides a solid foundation for meeting regulations like GDPR and CCPA.

* **Accountability & Transparency:** Embeds roles and responsibilities, risk assessments, and ongoing monitoring into the privacy program.
